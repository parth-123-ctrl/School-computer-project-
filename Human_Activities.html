One Health - One World

Human activities significantly impact ecosystems through various avenues, including pollution, deforestation, habitat destruction, climate change, and resource depletion. These impacts lead to biodiversity loss, disrupt ecological balance, and can have long-term consequences for the health of ecosystems and the planet.

Human activities significantly impact ecosystems, often negatively, through various means such as pollution, habitat destruction, and climate change. These actions disrupt ecological balance, reduce biodiversity, and can lead to the degradation of ecosystems and their ability to support life.
